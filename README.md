## ⚽ Football Culture & Fan Experiences

> Non-league (National League and below) match day culture — the 3 A's, traditions, fan voices, and community connections that make the lower tiers special.

### The 3 A's

| Principle | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Affordability** | 4–8× cheaper than the Premier League. A full away day costs £12–25 vs £50–148 at the top flight. |
| **Accessibility** | Walk-on gates, no ticket lotteries. Just turn up and pay. Stand wherever you like. |
| **Accountability** | Volunteer-run clubs where the chairman sits next to you and the manager is in the bar at full time. |
