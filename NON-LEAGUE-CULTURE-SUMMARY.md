# Non-League Match Day Culture — Research Summary

> Community-driven research on National League and non-league football match day traditions, culture, and fan experiences (2024–2026). Dedicated to the public domain.

## The 3 A's Framework

Non-league match days are defined by **Affordability**, **Accessibility**, and **Accountability**.

- **4–8× cheaper** than the Premier League (£12–25 vs £50–148 per match day)
- Walk-on gates, no ticket lotteries — anyone can turn up and play
- Chairman sits beside you; manager pints with fans at full time

## 13 Core Match Day Traditions

1. **The Pub Signal** — Pre-match pub gathering; scarf on the doorknob, chatter and predictions
2. **The Walk to the Ground** — Communal stroll through town, scarves raised, voices raised
3. **The Turnstile Ritual** — Paper programme (£2–4) and ticket at the gate
4. **The Physical Programme** — A5 keepsake with local history, line-ups, manager notes
5. **Pie, Mash & Gravy** — "Footy Scran" — steak-and-ale pie with gravy and mushy peas
6. **The Social Club** — Volunteer-run canteen where fans, officials, and players mingle as equals
7. **The Terraces** — Open standing, change ends at half-time, hear every tackle
8. **The Manager's Half-Time Chat** — Direct, intimate address to the terrace
9. **The Post-Match Digestion** — Lingering to replay key moments and discuss decisions
10. **The Pub Finish** — Post-game debate over pints; a 4–6 hour ritual in total
11. **The Away Day Reception** — Visiting supporters welcomed with open arms; FSA Away Day Award
12. **The Community Connection** — The club IS the community; you are part of the story
13. **The Loyalty Cycle** — Through relegations, half-empty grounds; every success is a group victory

## Top 5 Recommended 2026 Away Days

1. **Falmouth Town (Bickland Park)** — FSA 2025 Winner; Cornish pasties, hillside ground, holiday atmosphere
2. **FC Halifax Town (The Shay)** — 14k capacity, Football League feel, walkable town
3. **Torquay United (Plainmoor)** — English Riviera, beach + football, lively terraces
4. **Farnham Town (Memorial Ground)** — Town-centre location, innovative pricing, quality food
5. **Lewes FC (The Dripping Pan)** — South Downs footfall, craft beer, community-run
