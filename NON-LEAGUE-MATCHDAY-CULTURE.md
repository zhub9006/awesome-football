## 13 Core Match Day Traditions

1. Pub Signal - Pre-match gathering at the local pub
2. Walk to the Ground - Communal stroll with singing and banter
3. Turnstile Ritual - Buying programme and ticket
4. Turnstile Line - Social queuing at the gate
5. Pie, Mash & Gravy - Culinary centrepiece, Footy Scran
6. Social Club / Clubhouse - Pre- and post-match hub
7. The Terraces - Open standing, next to the pitch
8. Manager's Half-Time Chat - Direct address from the dugout
9. Post-Match Digestion - Staying to replay key moments
10. Pub Finish - After-game pints extend beyond 90 minutes
11. Away Day Reception - Welcoming visiting supporters
12. Community Connection - The club IS the community
13. Loyalty Cycle - Through ups and downs, fans stay loyal
